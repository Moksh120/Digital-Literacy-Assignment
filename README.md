## Project Overview 

This Digital Literacy Portfolio has been created as part of the Digital Literacy course to showcase key skills needed in the modern digital world. The project emphasizes awareness of responsible technology use, professional communication, building an online presence, and maintaining cyber security.

Through a range of organized activities, the portfolio demonstrates practical understanding of digital tools, collaboration platforms, coding practice, and ethical behavior online. It also represents the role of a Student Digital Ambassador in supporting and guiding peers to use digital technologies in a safe and effective manner.
