# Checklist for Responsible Social Media Use
## Do’s
•	Think before you post and ensure your content is respectful and appropriate. 

•	Use strong and unique passwords for your social media accounts. 

•	Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation. 

•	Maintain privacy settings and limit the sharing of personal details. 

•	Use social media for learning, networking, and skill development.


## Don’ts

•	Do not share confidential or sensitive personal information publicly. 

•	Avoid engaging in cyberbullying, trolling, or online arguments. 

•	Do not accept friend or follow requests from unknown or suspicious accounts. 

•	Avoid spending excessive time on social media that affects studies or health. 

•	Do not post inappropriate photos, comments, or content that can harm your reputation.
